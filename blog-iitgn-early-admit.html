IIT Gandhinagar Early Admit MTech Program 2025-26

Published on: February 14, 2025 | Author: Ataul Mustafa

Program Highlights

  • No GATE Score Required: GATE score is not mandatory for admission.
  • Fellowship: INR 15,400 per month (for candidates with a valid GATE score) or INR 6,000 (for others).
  • Travel Support: Up to INR 60,000 for attending international conferences.
  • Early Start: Selected candidates may complete their 8th semester of BTech at IITGN as a visiting student.

Disciplines Offered

  • Artificial Intelligence
  • Biological Engineering
  • Chemical Engineering
  • Civil Engineering
  • Computer Science & Engineering
  • Earth System Sciences
  • Electrical Engineering
  • Integrated Circuit Design and Technology
  • Materials Engineering
  • Mechanical Engineering

Eligibility Criteria

  • For NITs: Final year BTech students with a CPI/CGPA of not less than 8.0 (General/EWS/OBC) or 7.5 (SC/ST/PwD) at the end of their 3rd year.
  • For Select Colleges: Top 5 rank holders (General/EWS/OBC) or top 10 rank holders (SC/ST/PwD) in their department at the end of their 3rd year.
  • For IITs: Final year BTech students with a CPI/CGPA of not less than 6.0 at the end of their 3rd year.

Application Deadline

The last date to apply is March 26, 2025, 5:00 PM.

How to Apply

For more information and to apply, visit the official website: iitgn.ac.in/admissions/eamtech.
